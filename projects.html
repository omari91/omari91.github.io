Kinangop Wind Power Integration

A dynamic voltage stability analysis incorporating Kinangop wind power into Kenya’s 59-bus national grid, with simulations modeled in DIgSILENT PowerFactory. Read the full technical report.

The Challenge

Kenya's power grid needed to accommodate the intermittent nature of wind power from the Kinangop Wind Farm while maintaining system stability. The challenge was ensuring that voltage fluctuations from wind generation wouldn't destabilize the entire network serving millions of Kenyans.

My Approach

I developed a comprehensive dynamic voltage stability analysis using DIgSILENT PowerFactory, modeling the entire Kenyan power system with the proposed wind integration. The key was understanding that grid stability isn't just about technical parameters—it's about ensuring reliable power reaches communities that depend on it for healthcare, education, and economic development.

Predictive Maintenance AI for Geothermal Plants

Machine learning system for predicting equipment failures in geothermal power generation. View full documentation.

The Challenge

Geothermal power plants in Kenya's Rift Valley were experiencing unexpected equipment failures, leading to costly downtime and reduced clean energy generation. Traditional maintenance schedules were inefficient and reactive rather than preventive.

My Solution

I developed a predictive maintenance system using machine learning algorithms to analyze sensor data across key equipment. The system learned to identify patterns that preceded failures, enabling proactive maintenance scheduling.

Health Tech Load Balancing System

Applying power grid principles to optimize healthcare service distribution across rural Kenya

The Challenge

Rural health facilities in Kenya were experiencing uneven patient loads, with some overwhelmed while others remained underutilized. This led to longer wait times, reduced quality of care, and inefficient resource allocation.

My Innovation

Drawing from my power grid experience, I developed a load balancing algorithm that treats patient flow like electrical current. The system dynamically redistributes patients to optimize resource utilization while minimizing travel distance and wait times.
